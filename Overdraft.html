Eligibility Criteria

To qualify for a Project Finance at Capital Care Finance Company, you must meet the following criteria:

  • The project should be viable and have a strong revenue model.
  • Sponsors should have a proven track record in project execution.
  • A detailed feasibility report, including financial projections, must be submitted.
  • Compliance with legal, regulatory, and environmental norms is required.

Required Documentation

For Salaried Individuals:

  • Identity Proof (Aadhaar/PAN/Passport)
  • Address Proof
  • Last 3 months' salary slips
  • Bank statements for the last 6 months
  • Form 16 or Income Tax Returns

For Self-Employed:

  • Business registration documents
  • Income Tax Returns for last 2 years
  • Profit & Loss statements
  • Business bank statements
